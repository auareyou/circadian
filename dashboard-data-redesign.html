❌ Problems with current data

  • "80%" gauge with no denominator — 80% of what?
  • Two cards both labeled "Session count"
  • "Avg. light score" is meaningless to patients
  • Bar chart shows activity but no trajectory or story
  • "You're doing excellent!" feels hollow without proof
  • No actionable guidance — what should I change?
  • Sleep data disconnected from treatment context

✅ What the redesign does

  • Journey map: "9 of 14 days done, 4 left" — clear finish line
  • Each day is visible — partial vs full vs missed (Hooked: investment)
  • Quality broken into things patients control: time, positioning, consistency
  • Coaching nudge with specific, data-backed recommendation
  • Heatmap reveals patterns patient might not notice ("Wednesdays are hard")
  • Trend line shows improvement over time (SuperBetter: epic wins)
  • Every metric answers: Am I on track? Am I doing it right? What should I adjust?
