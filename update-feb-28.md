## User Journey

Mapped out the full patient experience in three parts:

- **Day 1:** Everything the patient does when they first get the iPad — setting up the app, answering a short questionnaire, positioning the light, and picking their alarm time. We're front-loading this because that's when people are most motivated.
- **Daily Sessions:** What happens every morning after Day 1 — alarm goes off, they answer one quick question, then watch their content while the light does its thing. The goal is to make this as easy as possible.
- **Last Day:** How we wrap things up. A clear, warm ending that feels intentional — not just "you're done, bye."

## What's In and Out of Scope

- Building a visual timeline that shows exactly what we're responsible for designing
- Our scope starts the moment the patient already has the iPad and ends when their last session is complete. Everything before (enrollment, shipping) and after (returning the device) is outside our scope.
