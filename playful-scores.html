Playful Score Explorations

Six ways to show session/treatment score. Each concept shown at three states: high (85%+), medium (55-70%), and low/starting (under 40%). The score should always feel encouraging — even a low score should feel like a beginning, never a failure.

01 The Garden — Your sessions grow things

Each completed session grows a flower. Partial sessions grow smaller. The garden fills over the treatment. Patients feel ownership — "I grew that." From SuperBetter: framing progress as something alive you're nurturing.

06 The Weather — Today's treatment forecast

Score expressed as a weather metaphor — ties directly to light therapy. Sunny = great session, partly cloudy = decent, overcast = needs improvement. Includes a "forecast" showing recent days. No weather is bad weather — even cloudy days have light. Gentle, familiar, non-numerical for patients who find scores stressful.
